    <CardGroup cols={1}>
      <Card title="The Goal" subtitle="Eliyahu Goldratt, 1984">
        Reads as a novel. Teaches constraint-based operations thinking faster than any textbook. Still the most widely assigned book in operations management courses at MIT and Wharton. **Start here.**
      </Card>
      <Card title="The Experience Trap" subtitle="Kishore Sengupta, Tarek Abdel-Hamid & Luk Van Wassenhove · HBR, 2008 · Free via Google Scholar">
        One of Sengupta's most cited papers on why experience doesn't always reduce project failure. Core to the SMP's complexity module. Search "Sengupta project complexity Cambridge" on Google Scholar.
      </Card>
      <Card title="The Fifth Discipline" subtitle="Peter Senge, 1990, updated 2006">
        The MIT Sloan foundational text on systems thinking and learning organizations. Directly relevant to understanding how complexity compounds in organizations.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
