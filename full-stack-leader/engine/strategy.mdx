<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Anchor texts">
    <CardGroup cols={1}>
      <Card title="Good Strategy, Bad Strategy" subtitle="Richard Rumelt, 2011">
        The clearest writing on what strategy actually is. His kernel framework — diagnosis, guiding policy, coherent actions — cuts through most of what gets called strategy in organizations. **Start here.**
      </Card>
      <Card title="Playing to Win" subtitle="Roger Martin & A.G. Lafley, 2013">
        A concise framework for making real strategic choices: where to play, how to win. Written by a former P&G CEO. Directly applicable.
      </Card>
      <Card title='"What Is Strategy?"' subtitle="Michael Porter · Harvard Business Review, 1996 · Free via JSTOR">
        Still the most rigorous single piece on the subject. Read after Rumelt — Porter is more analytical, Rumelt more practical.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Tab>
