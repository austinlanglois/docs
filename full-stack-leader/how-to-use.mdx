---
title: How to use this curriculum
description: Six months. Three parts. One concept per week applied to something real.
---

Each part maps to roughly two months. Read the anchor texts. Supplement with the papers. Apply one concept per week to something real in your work. Use the multimedia resources for exposure -- not as substitutes for the reading.

The Leadership Thread runs throughout all six months. Treat it as a practice, not a module.

  <Card title="Months 1-2" subtitle="Part 1: Steel">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Months 3-4" subtitle="Part 2: Signal">
    Navigating disruption. Focus on AI strategy and innovation first.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Months 5-6" subtitle="Part 3: Soul">
    The human architecture. This is where most leaders have the biggest gaps.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  The Leadership Thread runs the whole time. Don't save it for the end.
