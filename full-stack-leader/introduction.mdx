## What this is

You didn't get into an Ivy League. Neither did most of the people running things.

Here's the thing about elite executive education. The institutions aren't selling knowledge. They're selling access, credentialing, and three weeks in a hotel with other people who can afford to be away. The knowledge itself — the books, frameworks, case studies, research, lectures — most of it is already out there.

This curriculum pulls it together.

Six months. Three parts. Built on the architecture of the world's best senior management programmes — Cambridge, Harvard, Stanford, Wharton, MIT, INSEAD, Columbia, Yale — with every anchor text, supplemental resource, and multimedia source identified and sourced. No application. No tuition. No two-year wait.
