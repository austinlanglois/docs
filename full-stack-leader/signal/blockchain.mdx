<Warning>
  The blockchain space has a chronic signal-to-noise problem. Most books are either too technical, too speculative, or already outdated. What follows is what holds up for an executive audience. Treat blockchain as a specific solution to specific trust and provenance problems — not as a general strategic imperative.
</Warning>

    - Blockchain Research Institute free sector reports — blockchainresearchinstitute.org. Current use cases in supply chain, financial services, and healthcare

    - MIT OpenCourseWare, "Blockchain and Money" with Gary Gensler — full lecture series free on YouTube. The most rigorous free resource on blockchain for a business audience
