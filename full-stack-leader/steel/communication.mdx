title: Executive communication and storytelling

<Note>
  This module covers what most management self-study leaves out. Elite business schools treat communication as a core management skill, not a soft one. Columbia's required MBA curriculum includes Leadership Communication. Stanford GSB has a dedicated communication track. HBS treats executive presence as inseparable from leadership effectiveness.
</Note>

      <Card title="Resonate" subtitle="Nancy Duarte, 2010">
