---
title: Executive communication and storytelling
description: Strategy and insight are only as powerful as your ability to communicate them.
---

<Note>
  Elite business schools treat communication as a core management skill, not a soft one. Columbia's required MBA curriculum includes Leadership Communication. Stanford GSB has a dedicated communication track. HBS treats executive presence as inseparable from leadership effectiveness.
</Note>
